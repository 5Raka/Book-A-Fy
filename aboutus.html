About Us

Gov Forge is a software designed to provide quality services to the Government and to the public, with the latest technology available in the market. It enables easier access to citizens and provides a one-stop channel for businesses and citizens to communicate with government agencies and ministries.It is a platform as a whole which helps the citizens to avail beneficiaries from the government through this website.It will also help the businesses and citizens to access the sevices quickly and easily.



OUR GOALS

USER-FRIENDLY

Easy to use,navigate, and understand for travellers who are searching for information,planning and booking trips. Govforge also focuses on providing clear and concise information about destinations,flights, hotels and other activities.

SECURITY

Ensuring safe and secure transactions for booking tickets.GovForge also focuses on maintaining the confidentiality of user's personal information and to protect it from unauthorized access.

HASSLE-FREE EXPERIENCE

Aims to provide a seamless and hassle free experience to passengers while booking tickets,planning their trip,and accessing various sevices.

Services We Provide


Ticket Booking:The passengers can reserve a seat on a flight for a specific date,time and route.This can be easily done online through our website.The passengers need to provide information such as destination,travel date,preffered train and class of travel. They can also cancel their tickets .The amount of refund will depend on the timing of cancellation and class of travel.

Holiday Package:This typically refers to a pre-planeed travel itinerary that includes flights,accomodations and other activities,such as tours and excursions,all bundled together in one package.This will make it easier for travellers to plan and book their vacation without having to arrange each component individually.

Hotel Booking:People can reserve a room or suite in a hotel for a specific datee or range of dates.They need to provide information such s destination,chech-in and check-out dates, number of guests, and room preference respectively.The website will then display a list of available hotels that match their criteria,along with room rates and other amenities.
